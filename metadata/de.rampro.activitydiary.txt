Summary:Log and plan your recurring activities
Description:
Activity Dairy allows to record a diary for for your activities. You can
categorize them and get a reminder for recurrent actions.

This app is currently under development and far from being feature complete. Do
not use in a productive environment and expect your data to be lost on version
upgrade. Please be patient or contribute to the
[https://github.com/ramack/ActivityDiary github project] and submit bug reports,
feature requestsand pull requests!
