Data entry app for the field record LASK

You can find LASK at [https://codeberg.org/ibinshoid/LASK]

With LASKmobile actions can be entered on the mobile phone, and then compared with the PC version.

Questions, suggestions and exchange of views on LASK and LASKmobile:

- in the forum at http://www.agrarforum.de/open-source/vorstellung-ackerschlagkartei-lask-t2451.html

- as an issue at [https://codeberg.org/ibinshoid/LASKmobile/issues]
