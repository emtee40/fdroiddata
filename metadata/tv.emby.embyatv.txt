Description:
Bringing all of your personal media together into one place has never been
easier! Emby unites your personal videos, music, and photos and streams them to
your devices.

This app is free and will function for browsing your collection. For playback,
you need to either unlock the app with a small in-app purchase or have an active
Emby Premiere membership.

Get the free, open source Emby Server at [http://emby.media] (required as part
of your Emby install). You must be on Emby Server 3.0.5572.0+

• Automatically converts your media on-the-fly to play on any device. •
Organizes your media into an elegant display with artwork, rich metadata and
related content. • Easily share your media with friends and family. • Rich
parental control and management features allow you to easily control access for
your entire family. • Stream your cloud-synced media (with installed cloud sync
providers) • Watch Live TV and manage your DVR (with installed live tv provider)

Energize your collection with Emby and take your media to the next level.
