Spinster Android is the officiel Android client for Spinster, the women-first social network server.

• Material Design
• Most Mastodon APIs implemented
• Multi-Account support
• Dark and light theme with the possibility to auto-switch based on the time of day
• Drafts - compose posts and save them for later
• Choose between different emoji styles 
• Optimized for all screen sizes
• Completely open-source - no non-free dependencies like Google services

Spinster Android is based on Tusky.

To learn more about Spinster, visit https://spinster.xyz/.
